Welcome to ByteTheNorm

ByteTheNorm was born out of a desire to empower professionals who feel out of place in traditional tech settings. We believe that diversity of thought, lived experiences, and leadership styles drive innovation. Our journey began when we recognized how often norms in the industry stifle authenticity and brilliance. Through events, mentoring, and training, we help people break barriers not just fit in, but stand out.

Whether you're a developer, designer, manager, or dreamer ByteTheNorm exists to help you soar.
